Cruise Door Decal Studio

The on-screen door is shown at a proportional scale so you can compose your layout. Only the printed A4 sheet at 100% is true-to-size. Drop decals on the door, drag and resize them, then print the cutout sheet to scale.

Decals

Tap a decal to add it to the door.

Output

Print the layout as a true-to-size A4 cutout sheet. Set your printer to 100% and turn off Fit to Page.

Represented door: about 66 cm wide by 190 cm tall. The screen preview is proportional only - sizes and positions are stored in centimetres. The printed sheet at 100% (Fit to Page off) is the only true-to-size output.
