Cruise Door Decal Studio

Decorate a stateroom door, then print a true-to-size A4 cutout sheet. Tap Decals to add art, then drag, resize and remove pieces on the door.

The on-screen door is proportional only. Sizes are stored in centimetres; the printed A4 sheet at 100% (Fit to Page off) is the only true-to-size output. The circular plate up top is the room number - tap it to edit, and decals stay clear of it.

Add a decal

Tap any decal to drop it on the door. Add as many as you like, then close to arrange them.
